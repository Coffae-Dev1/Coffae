About Coffaé

Small space.
Full life.

We design furniture for the way people actually live today — in apartments measured in square feet, not square meters, where every piece has to earn its place.

The Problem

Great furniture shouldn't require a great floor plan.

Moving into your first apartment means making hard choices. Most furniture is designed for houses — oversized, single-purpose, built for space you don't have. The rest is cheap and disposable, designed to last exactly as long as your lease.

We started Coffaé because neither option felt right. Young people deserve furniture that's considered, well-made, and actually built for small spaces — without the showroom price tag.

Our Answer

Every piece does more than one thing.

The Coffaé approach is simple: constraint is the best design brief. Every product we make nests, slides, folds, or doubles — not as a gimmick, but as a genuine design solution. Less clutter. More intention.

We use real materials — solid walnut, tempered glass, quality upholstery — because furniture you keep for ten years is more sustainable than furniture you replace every two.

01

Space-first design

Every dimension, every mechanism, every fold is decided with one question: does this earn its footprint? If it doesn't, we redesign until it does.

02

Built to last

We don't make throwaway furniture. Solid hardwoods, tempered glass, and hardware-free joints mean our pieces move with you — apartment to apartment, city to city.

03

Honest pricing

Quality furniture at a price that doesn't require a second job. We cut the showroom markup, not the craftsmanship.
